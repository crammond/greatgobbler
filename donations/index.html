The 22nd Annual Great Gobbler is on Thursday, November 28 2024! Registration is open! Sign up now to secure your spot and receive a Gobbler shirt, available for the first 500 registrants only!

Donations

Each year we accept donations of canned goods for the Nashua Soup Kitchen and Shelter. Donations of canned goods for the Nashua Soup Kitchen and Shelter will be accepted on Wednesday, November 27th 10:00am to 5:00pm AND race day morning in the Nashua South cafeteria. Thank you for your generosity!

Monetary donations to the Nashua Cross Country and Track programs will also be accepted through the registration page and in-person at bib pickup and race day.
